About SkyWatch

SkyWatch is an all-in-one weather app that delivers reliable and real-time weather updates using the OpenWeatherMap API. Our goal is to keep you informed about the weather—no matter where you are. Built with the latest web technologies, SkyWatch is entirely free to use. Whether you're traveling, monitoring air quality, or simply want to know the forecast, SkyWatch is your go-to weather companion.

SkyWatch provides accurate, real-time weather information for locations worldwide. Our mission is to help you stay prepared for any weather conditions.
